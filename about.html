Pronunciation: /suːrjəˈʃaŋkər dʒɔɪ/ (preferred: Soorya)

Bio

I build close-to-the-metal systems: C programming, microcontrollers, digital logic, circuit design, and performance analysis. I currently serve as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for ECE 2k7 (Electrical Engineering Fundamentals Laboratory), helping students translate theory into real circuits and measurements.

I’m most interested in the layers beneath abstractions—where timing, memory behavior, and signal integrity determine whether something works reliably.
